project: Mortimer Street

Office fit out in the heart of London, near Oxford Circus.

OVERVIEW

TWorks involved 8,000 square feet of CAT A+ Fit-out and 5,500 square feet of CAT B.  This was split over 2 existing floors and an additional top floor open plan office space, with roof top.

The work was delivered while the existing building remained fully occupied.  The project achieved a BREEAM "Excellent" rating, highlighting its commitment to environmental responsibility.

SCOPE OF SERVICES

  • New LV panel board and distribution systems
  • Containment
  • Emergency lighting
  • Lighting installation including feature pendants to break out zones and general office luminaires with lighting control
  • Installation of low-level power including cleaners sockets and floor boxes
  • Earthing and bonding
  • Adaption of the existing fire alarm
  • Lift supply
  • Access control system
  • Audio / video door entry system
  • Security system
  • Data and telecommunications cable
  • Power supplies to mechanical plant including lifts & chillers

project: Wyboston Lakes, Waterfront

Overhaul of hotel electrical services in Bedfordshire

OVERVIEW

The project involved a complete overhaul of the hotel spa, restaurant & bar, private meeting rooms and internal and external communal areas at Wyboston Lakes resort.

Working with the GPF Lewis Projects Division the team designed and installed the new electrical layout in each area. The works was carried out in three phases to minimise disruption to the venue's operations.

SCOPE OF SERVICES

  • Emergency lighting
  • Lighting installation with lighting control
  • General power
