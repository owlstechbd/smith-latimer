our services

Founded in XXXX, Smith and Latimer is one of London and the South East's leading electrical contractors, specialising in high-quality electrical installation and maintenance services. Our reputation for reliability and excellence has allowed us to become a trusted partner for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the region.

LV Infrastructure

We specialise in the design, installation, and maintenance of Low Voltage (LV) infrastructure, providing reliable power distribution for both residential and commercial properties. Our experienced team ensures that your LV systems are compliant with all relevant UK regulations.

Internal and External Lighting Solutions

Our lighting solutions enhance the aesthetic and functional aspects of any environment.

  • Internal Lighting: Customised lighting installations that improve visibility and energy efficiency for homes and businesses.
  • External Lighting: Security and decorative outdoor lighting options that illuminate pathways, gardens, and building exteriors, enhancing safety and curb appeal.
Life Safety Systems

We design and install advanced life safety systems to protect people and property. Our comprehensive solutions include:

  • Fire Alarm Systems: Tailored fire detection and alarm systems that meet regulatory requirements and ensure a swift response during emergencies.
  • Smoke Extract Systems with Motorised Dampers: Effective systems designed to control smoke and heat during a fire, improving occupant safety and assisting emergency services.
Disabled Refuge and Alarm Systems

We are committed to ensuring accessibility for all. Our installations include:

  • Disabled Refuge Systems: Reliable communication systems that allow disabled individuals to call for assistance during emergencies.
  • Disabled Alarms: Alarms designed specifically to alert and assist individuals with disabilities in critical situations.
Access Control Systems

Our access control solutions enhance security by regulating entry to restricted areas. We design and install advanced systems, including keycard entry, biometric scanners, and intercom systems, ensuring safety and convenience for your premises.

Security Systems & CCTV

We offer the installation and maintenance of high-quality security systems, including CCTV surveillance systems. Our services encompass:

  • CCTV Systems: Customised installations that deter criminal activity and provide peace of mind through comprehensive monitoring.
  • Integrated Security Systems: Comprehensive solutions that combine alarms, monitoring, and access control for a robust security setup tailored to your needs.
PV Array (Photovoltaic)

In line with our commitment to sustainability, we design and install photovoltaic (PV) arrays. Our solar energy solutions help clients reduce their carbon footprint and energy costs while harnessing clean, renewable energy.

Power Solutions

Our power solutions encompass everything from basic installations to complex electrical systems. We provide:

  • Power Distribution: Efficient distribution systems designed to maximise energy usage and minimise downtime.
  • Equipment and Machinery Connections: Safe and reliable connections for various electrical equipment, ensuring optimal performance and operational efficiency.
